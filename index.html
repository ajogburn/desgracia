What he does

STYLES

20+ years across the styles that matter. Custom work in any of these — or a mix of them.

Realism

Black & grey and color realism — portraits, animals, and detail-heavy pieces that read like photos on skin.

Japanese

Samurai, oni, and Eastern-inspired work with bold structure, flow, and traditional motifs.

Traditional

Classic American traditional and neo-trad energy — bold lines, solid fill, roses, crosses, and icons that age well.

Fine Line

Delicate linework for florals, landscapes, geometric accents, and pieces that stay clean and readable.

Portrait

Faces, figures, and character work with depth and shading — built for likeness and impact.

Lettering

Script, names, and quotes — custom lettering that sits right with the rest of the piece.

Blackwork

Solid black, high-contrast compositions, and graphic pieces that hold up from a distance.

Illustrative

Story-driven designs — animals, scenes, and custom art that sits between realism and flash.

Color

Full color, soft color accents, and blended work when the design needs more than black & grey.
